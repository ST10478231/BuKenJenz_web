Welcome to Empowering the Nation!

Empowering the Nation was established in 2022 and offers courses in Johannesburg. Hundreds of domestic workers and gardeners have been trained on both the six-month long Learnerships and six-week Short Skills training Programmes to empower themsevkes and can provide more marketable skills.

About Us

Empowering the Nation is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing high-quality education and training to domestic workers and gardeners in Johannesburg. Our mission is to empower individuals with the skills and knowledge they need to improve their living and contribute positively to their communities.

We offer a range of courses, including six-month Learnerships and six-week Short Skills training Programmes, designed to equip you with practical skills that are in demand in the job market.
